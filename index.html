About Maldives

Maldives consists of a chain of over 1,200 small coral islands in the Indian Ocean, 600-1,000 miles (965-1,610 km) west of Sri Lanka. It is the smallest, independent country in Asia, and was a British protectorate until 1965. Most of the population of 254,000 lives on 200 or so of the islands
